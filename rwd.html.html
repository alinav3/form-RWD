What is RWD?

Responsive web design (RWD) helps users view your website on any device they have. Basically, it allows your website to adapt and be accessible for all your clients. The number of devices is constantly growing and they come in a wide range of sizes, from the tiniest like Smart Watches, to huge (more than 250 inches) TVs.
